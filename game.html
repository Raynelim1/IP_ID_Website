Game Overview

Lab Breakout is an educational game designed for secondary school students studying chemistry. Apply real-world chemical principles to solve complex puzzles and unlock the secrets of each room.

Learning Objectives

By completing Lab Breakout, students will:

  • Understand and apply exothermic reaction principles
  • Master pH scales and acid-base neutralization
  • Learn oxidation and reduction reactions
  • Explore chemiluminescence and light-producing reactions
  • Develop problem-solving skills in a practical chemistry context
  • Apply theoretical knowledge to real-world chemical scenarios

Scoring and Progress

Your performance is tracked by the time taken to complete all four rooms. The faster you solve the puzzles using correct chemistry principles, the better your score. Check the leaderboard to see how you rank among your peers and compete globally!
