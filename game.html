The Game

Game Overview

Lab Breakout is an educational game designed for secondary school students studying chemistry. Navigate through four interconnected rooms in a chemistry lab, each presenting a unique challenge based on real chemical principles.

The 4 Rooms

Room 1: Ice Melting

Learn about exothermic reactions by selecting appropriate chemicals to generate heat and melt the ice block protecting the key.

Difficulty: Beginner

Concept: Exothermic Reactions and Thermochemistry

Room 2: Acid Neutralization

Neutralize acidic floor conditions by calculating and mixing the correct base solution. Find the optimal pH balance to safely retrieve the key.

Difficulty: Intermediate

Concept: Acid-Base Reactions and pH

Room 3: Lock Corrosion

Perform a redox reaction to corrode the lock mechanism. Choose the right combination of oxidizing and reducing agents.

Difficulty :Advanced

Concept: Redox Reactions and Electrochemistry

Room 4: Light Production

Create chemiluminescence to light up the dark room. Combine reactants to produce visible light and navigate to the final key.

Difficulty: Expert

Concept: Chemiluminescence and Light Emission

Learning Objectives

By completing Lab Breakout, students will:

  • Understand and apply exothermic reaction principles
  • Master pH scales and acid-base neutralization
  • Learn oxidation and reduction reactions
  • Explore chemiluminescence and light-producing reactions
  • Develop problem-solving skills in a practical chemistry context
  • Apply theoretical knowledge to real-world chemical scenarios

Scoring and Time Tracking

Your performance is tracked by the time taken to complete all four rooms. The faster you solve the puzzles using correct chemistry, the better your score. Check the leaderboard to see how you rank among your peers!
