How It Works

Step into an immersive VR environment and use your knowledge of chemistry to escape four challenging rooms. Each room tests a different chemical concept:

  • Room 1: Exothermic Reactions - Use heat to melt ice
  • Room 2: Acid Neutralization - Balance pH levels
  • Room 3: Redox Reactions - Corrode the lock
  • Room 4: Light Production - Illuminate the path
