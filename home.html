How It Works

Step into an immersive VR environment and use your knowledge of chemistry to escape four challenging rooms. Each room tests a different chemical concept:

  • Room 1: Ice Melting (Exothermic Reactions)
  • Room 2: Acid Neutralization (pH Balancing)
  • Room 3: Lock Corrosion (Redox Reactions)
  • Room 4: Light Production (Photochemistry)
