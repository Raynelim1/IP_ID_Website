Ice Melting

Generate enough heat with the correct exothermic reaction to melt the ice block and recover the key.

In-Depth Room Explanation

This room introduces exothermic reactions. You must identify reagents that release heat quickly but safely. The puzzle tests reaction selection and sequence, not random mixing.

  • Identify which chemicals are heat-producing when combined
  • Estimate whether the reaction output is enough to melt the ice
  • Avoid low-energy combinations that waste time

Clear strategy: Start with the strongest safe exothermic pair, then monitor the ice change indicator before making a second mix.
